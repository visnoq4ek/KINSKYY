YAROSLAV KINSKY

PULSE OF THE NEBULAEP

(INCL, LUKEA REMIX)

Pulse Of The Nebula EP opens with an uplifting and energy-filled Synthetic Dreams designed with progressive elements interwoven with constantly evolving aspects, including atmospheric pads, immersive textures, spacey melody, and a powerful bassline, creating a steady yet dynamic musical journey.

Staying within his recognizable sound, Yaroslav continues his experiments bringing new ideas to life. Filled with electricity and vintage synth patterns, Voltage Voyage presents its solid broken beat infused with layers of modulated sound.

Rounding out the originals, the title track Pulse Of The Nebula continues the journey with its steadypaced rhythm, dreamy pads, melancholy melody, and airy background textures, guiding listeners on a mediative voyage of sensitivity and thoughtfulness.

Closing the release, Lukea dives deeper with his remix, delivering a hypnotic minimalistic atmosphere with progressive arrangements and mysterious voices, bringing an enigmatic tone to the original track. The musical mind has a refined pulsating soul. If you feel the same way, we are of the Same Mind. Released April 20, 2023
